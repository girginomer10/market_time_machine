# Code of Conduct

Market Time Machine is an open source project for learning, research, and careful historical analysis. The community should be serious about accuracy without being hostile to people.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but correct mistakes clearly.
- Separate critique of an idea from critique of a person.
- Credit sources and contributors.
- Be transparent about uncertainty, especially around market history and data licensing.
- Help new contributors understand the project's standards.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Dismissing licensing, data provenance, or future-leakage concerns.
- Misrepresenting the project as investment advice, a broker, or a recommendation engine.
- Repeatedly derailing technical or historical review.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code. Maintainers may also temporarily or permanently restrict participation.

If you need to report a concern, open a private channel with the maintainer rather than escalating in public threads when possible.
